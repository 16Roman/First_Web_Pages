Dog Hygiene

Bathing Your Dog

Dogs will always find a way to get dirty no matter what. So in order to avoid your house smelling bad, frequent baths are a must!

Grooming Your Dog

If left untouched, some dogs fur will knot up and cause pain for the dog.
Grooming will also help control your dog shedding, and you can check for harmful fleas and ticks in the process.
